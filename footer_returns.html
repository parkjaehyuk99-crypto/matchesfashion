  • How do I return an item?

    Returning an item is easy with our Returns portal. You can drop-off your return at a location or book a courier for home collection (this may incur a charge - please view our Return Policy).

    1. 1. Sign in as normal then go to your Order History and click the 'RETURN ITEMS' button next to the order you want to return. If you have placed your order via Guest Checkout, you will need to create an account.Go to order history
    2. 2. Select the products you want to return and then complete the short form choosing your return method.
    3. 3. Pack the items using the original packaging provided to wrap the items you are returning.
    4. 4. Print your return label if needed ensuring the previous delivery label is covered with the new returns label.
    5. 5. If using drop-off, take your package to the correct location or wait for home collection. You will be notified by email once your return has been received and processed.

    Still confused? Contact us for more help.

  • What is MATCHES' return policy?

    How to use our return process

    All returns should be sent back to us in their original packaging provided.

    Returning an item is easy with our Returns portal. You can drop-off your return at a location or book a courier for home collection (this may incur a charge - please view our Return Policy).

    You have 14 days from receipt of your order to request your return number. You will receive your return number by email. Unsuitable items must then be returned within 7 days of receiving your return number.

    To ensure your return is covered by our returns policy and does not encounter a delay, items must be returned using the original packaging provided and from the same country to which they were delivered. You can return using any secure means but we may not accept liability.

    The item(s) should be returned unworn and in perfect condition, with all MATCHES and designer garment tags still attached. Where provided, any designer packaging such as authenticity cards, dust bags, and leather tags should be included in your return.

    Footwear should be returned unmarked and in its original and undamaged packaging as this is considered part of the product.

    Briefs, swimming costumes, and bikini bottoms should be tried on over underwear. Returns may not be accepted if the strip has been removed (swimwear) or if the items are soiled, and may be sent back to the customer. Please note, for hygiene reasons, face masks are non-returnable. Returns that are damaged, soiled, or returned without their original labels may not be accepted and may be sent back to the customer.

    All orders that are returned within the 14 day return window will be refunded to the original payment method unless you select MATCHES credit when creating your return. If you paid with a combination of debit/credit card and gift card/credit, anything paid by debit/credit card will be refunded first, then the remaining amount will be credited to your MATCHES account.

    For orders shipping to Australia, Canada, China, Hong Kong, Japan, Macau, Singapore, South Korea, Taiwan and United States, sale and OUTLET items can be returned for refund or store credit unless they were purchased during a promotional period where a code was applied on top of a sale price or are clearance items.

    Clearance items are those discounted by 60% or more. Please note that clearance items are not eligible for return, exchange or credit unless faulty.

    Whilst we offer a flexible returns policy, we do monitor the number of returns and note any unusual returns activity. Unusual activity could be where items are returned which we suspect have been worn (not just tried on) or damaged, returns which have missing or incorrect items, or returning significantly more than we consider normal. This unusual activity or other breaches of returns policy could result in closure of your MATCHES account. We also reserve the right to take other appropriate action, depending on the particular circumstances, such as withholding issuing a refund or legal action.

  • Can I exchange an item?

    We do not offer exchanges as we cannot guarantee the availability of item(s) upon arrival of your return package.

    However, you are more than welcome to return the item you don't wish to keep for a full refund and then place a new order for the item that you want instead.

  • How do I get my return label or QR Code?

    After requesting a return, a return label or QR code is sent to your email address. If you can't find it in your inbox, access our returns portal by going to your Order History and clicking the 'RETURN ITEMS' button next to the order you want to return. Scroll to “Returns history for this order” and tap on the tracking number to retrieve your label.

    It may take up to 15 minutes for your return label to be sent to your email address.

    For DPD collection a label is not required, you will be sent a confirmation of your collection window via email and all you'll need to do is pass your return to the driver.

    If your label failed to generate in the returns portal, or you can't find it in your inbox, please contact us quoting your order number, which starts with “OMF”.

    Please note, this is only valid if you are returning from United Kingdom, Australia, Canada, China, Hong Kong, Singapore, South Korea or Taiwan.

    For other countries, please use the label provided in your original package. If you need another copy of your original label, you can request one via our digital assistant. Click on the live chat button on the bottom right to start.

  • How do I book a collection?

    To book a collection, sign into your account, go to Order History within My Account and select the items you wish to return. You will then see the available collection options depending on your country.
