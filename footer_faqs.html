  • How do I keep my account safe?

    In order to keep your MATCHES account safe, we recommend you follow the simple steps below.

    When setting your password, it must be at least 6 characters and contain at least one uppercase letter and a number. The longer your password is, the more secure it is.

    Use a unique password on MATCHES. Using a unique password will ensure that if your password is disclosed on another website, it cannot be used on MATCHES.

    Do not use the same password for your email accounts as you do on sites like MATCHES.

    Change your password periodically. While we do not require users to change passwords after a period of time, it is recommended to update your password periodically.

    Change your password if you suspect that any of the accounts you use online on any website has been compromised.

  • How will I know if you have received my order?

    When your credit card is authorised, funds are put on hold until the transaction is completed and we will send you a confirmation email that we have received your order. In the meantime we will prepare your order.

    Once your order is ready to be shipped, we will send you a confirmation via email that your order is ready to be shipped.

    When your order is shipped, your payment will be processed and we will send you a dispatch confirmation email containing your tracking details.

    Your billing information must be exactly as it appears on your credit card statement. If any numbers are different, the order cannot be processed. Be sure to correctly spell city names and avoid using abbreviations unless that is how your information appears on your statement.

    Contact your financial institution if you need the exact billing address associated with your credit card.

  • Is it safe to use my credit card on MATCHES?

    For your security, MATCHES may require you to enter your 3- or 4-digit card identification number (CID/CVN/CVV/CVC) on certain orders for Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover credit cards.

    For more information about our secure checkout, read our Privacy Policy.

  • Do I have to pay duty and import charges?

    Customers in Australia, Bahrain, Canada, China, South Korea, Japan, Kuwait, Saudi Arabia, Singapore, Switzerland, UAE, USA, and Qatar, items are shipped on a DDP (delivery duty paid) basis and all prices shown include the relevant import taxes and duties (where the individual product or total order value is over the relevant country duty threshold).

    If you are shipping to the US or in the European Union, Customs Duty has been pre-paid for you by MATCHES with no additional charges on delivery.

    For customers shipping to countries that are not listed above or if you have an opt-out at checkout, tax and duty will be charged upon the shipment's arrival in the destination country.

    Our delivery partner will contact you once your items are in customs to let you know the cost. This may delay your delivery time as goods held at customs are it's your responsibility to pay the necessary charges for local authorities to release the goods.

    Local sales tax will be charged on all orders at checkout in selected states where applicable. It is your responsibility as the customer to report any purchases of tangible personal property that have not been taxed by MATCHES and pay use tax on those purchases unless exempt under local state law.

    Please be aware that if you don't choose to pay the import taxes we reserve the right to charge any additional costs incurred by MATCHES to you.

    Please note, duty is applied as a flat-rate percentage of total item value for all products.

    Please note, for returns from orders placed on a DDP basis, duty and/or tax is only refunded if the order was shipped to Australia, Canada, Japan, Switzerland, Saudi Arabia, Bahrain, United Arab Emirates, Singapore or United States.

  • How do I track my order?

    After you place your order, you will receive an email acknowledging your order has been received.

    Once you have passed our fraud security check, you will receive a second email from us confirming your order.

    Within a 24-hour period, your items will be processed and dispatched. Please note, this can take longer during sales.

    Once your order has left our Distribution Centre, you will receive an email unique tracking number, so you can check the current status of your shipment.

    If you place an order and choose our same-day shipping service, you will receive a text notifying you of the status of your order.

  • What is Premium nominated time slot delivery?

    We have partnered with ADDISON LEE to offer a Premium delivery service to London addresses within the M25, allowing you to choose a date and 1-hour time slot for delivery, seven days a week.

    Same-day delivery for orders placed before 5pm Monday to Saturday and before 4pm on Sunday, or up to seven days ahead.

    If your order is eligible for Premium delivery, the option will be displayed to you at checkout.

    For orders placed using our Nominated Time Slot delivery, customers will receive an email containing a tracking link allowing them to track their delivery live, direct to their door, from one hour before the delivery is due.

  • Why are shipping restrictions applied to some products?

    Due to international trading agreements and regulations, MATCHES must adhere to some specific shipping restrictions.

    We are unable to ship exotic skins outside the UK. For any customers outside the UK wishing to purchase an item containing exotic skins, please contact Customer Care.

    In addition, we are required by UK regulation to obtain a CITES (Convention on International Trade in Endangered Species of Wild Fauna and Flora) permit before items made from exotic skins can be shipped.

    This may cause a delay in processing your order.

    Please note, if you are travelling you must carry your CITES permit at all times, otherwise, your item can be confiscated by the local customs.

    Fine jewellery cannot be dispatched to Russia. Customers in Russia wishing to purchase fine jewellery must arrange for their shipment to be delivered outside of the Russian Federation.
